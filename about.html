Our Mission

Health intelligence for people who want the truth.

We started Wello because we were frustrated. Frustrated by smartwatches that counted steps but couldn't tell you if you were overtrained. Frustrated by apps that sent 200 notifications a day but gave you zero understanding of your nervous system.

So we built something different. A screenless band with medical-grade sensors. An algorithm trained on peer-reviewed science. A morning summary so clear you know exactly what your body needs — in three numbers.

We are a team of biomedical engineers, clinicians, and product designers based in Lahore, Pakistan. We believe premium health technology should be accessible, honest, and built to last.

What we believe

Our principles.

Every product decision at Wello runs through the same filter: does this make the user's health more understandable?

Science, not trends

Every metric Wello tracks is grounded in peer-reviewed physiology. We don't add features because other devices have them — we add them because evidence says they matter.

Invisible by design

A health device shouldn't compete for your attention. Wello has no screen, no buzzes, no badges — just a silent sensor doing its job and a morning report when you're ready.

Your data, always

Your health data is yours. We never sell it, never use it for advertising, and give you a full export at any time. Wello is funded by device sales, not by your information.

No subscriptions

One purchase. Lifetime app access. Lifetime data storage. We believe charging monthly for a device you already bought is wrong — so we simply don't do it.

Built to last

Medical-grade components. Aerospace-grade ceramic coating. A strap engineered to survive sweat, showers, and sleep for years — not a product cycle.

Made in Pakistan

We're proud to be building world-class health technology from Lahore. Wello is proof that precision hardware and rigorous science can come from anywhere.
